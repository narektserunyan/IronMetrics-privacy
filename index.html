Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2025

IronMetrics is developed by Narek Tserunyan. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data securely.

What we collect

By default, the App does not collect personal data. Only weight and height is asked, but they are optional Your workout data (exercises, sets, reps, notes) is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

Data storage & retention

Your data is saved on your device. Deleting the App will remove locally stored data. If you use iCloud/Device backups, Apple may include App data in those backups according to your system settings.

Third-party services

The App uses analytics, advertising, but not tracking services.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 9 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Your rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, requests such as access, correction, or deletion can be fulfilled by you directly by editing or deleting data in the App or uninstalling it.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

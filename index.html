Last updated: July 04, 2026

IronMetrics is developed by Narek Tserunyan. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data securely.

What we collect

By default, the App does not collect personal data. The App may ask for optional information such as weight and height to calculate basic fitness-related metrics. Providing this information is optional.

Your workout data, including exercises, sets, reps, notes, progress, and training history, is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

Data storage & retention

Your data is saved on your device. Deleting the App will remove locally stored data. If you use iCloud/Device backups, Apple may include App data in those backups according to your system settings.
